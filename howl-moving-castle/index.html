Howl's Moving Castle

Howl's Moving Castle is a 2004 Japanese animated fantasy film written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki. Loosely based on the 1986 novel of the same name by British author Diana Wynne Jones, the film tells the story of Sophie, a young woman who is cursed by the Witch of the Waste to become an old woman. She flees to the Moving Castle, home to the wizard Howl, and becomes his assistant.
Director: Hayao Miyazaki
Writer: Hayao Miyazaki
Studio: Studio Ghibli

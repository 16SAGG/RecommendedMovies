My Neighbor Totoro

In 1958 Japan, university professor Tatsuo Kusakabe and his two daughters, Satsuki and Mei, move to an old house in the countryside to be closer to their hospitalized mother. While exploring the nearby woods, Mei encounters Totoro, a gigantic, friendly creature who resembles a cat mixed with a rabbit. Mei befriends Totoro and his smaller companions, and the two sisters soon find themselves enchanted by the magical world of the forest.
Director: Hayao Miyazaki
Writer: Hayao Miyazaki
Studio: Studio Ghibli
